• Connect4 is a simple and fun game for 2 players, that is played on a 6X7 grid.
  • Two players take turns. One is assigned red discs and the other yellow discs.
  • Players take turns dropping one of their colored discs from the top into any of the 7 columns.
  • The disc will occupy the lowest available spot within the chosen column.
  • The game ends when a player connects four of their discs in a row.
  • The connection can ne horizontal, vertical, diagonal, or antidiagonal.
  • The first player to achieve wins the game.
  • If all slots are filled and no player has four discs in a row, the game is draw.
